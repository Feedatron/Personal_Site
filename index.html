About Me

Hello! My name is Phil and I am a Game Developer turned Web Developer. I originally got into web development back mid 2021 when my now mentor was showing me some of his work that he has done. We sat there for hours looking through each line of code and I was hooked.

After High School, I moved up to Canada to pursue a career in Game Development. Numerous different coding languages, adobe, sound design and other things. Eventually I had unforseen circumstances and I had to put my schooling on hold and move back down to the states.

Fast forward to today with my love of programming and making things, I continued to work hard and better myself by taking many classes and online courses to help refine my skills as a developer.
